The Dangers of Overspeeding

Overspeeding is a leading cause of accidents on our roads. When drivers exceed the speed limit, they put themselves and others at risk. Our tracking devices help you identify and address overspeeding issues, reducing the risk of accidents and saving lives.

  • Number of accidents caused by overspeeding per year: 10,000
  • Number of lives lost due to overspeeding per year: 500
  • Economic cost of overspeeding-related accidents per year: $1 billion
